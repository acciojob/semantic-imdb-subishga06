Summary

"Inception" is a 2010 science fiction thriller directed by Christopher Nolan. The story follows Dom Cobb, a skilled thief who steals secrets from deep within the subconscious during the dream state. Cobb is offered a chance at redemption if he can successfully perform inception – planting an idea into someone’s mind. The film explores dreams, reality, and the complexities of the human mind.
